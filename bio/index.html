--- layout: default title: Adam McFillin - Bio ---

Adam McFillin is an artist currently involved with producing sound-based
performance and installation works that investigate protocol, power and structure in communications networks.

He has created scores for film, television and theatre, as well as
sound design for location-specific media artworks. He is an actively performing and recording musician.

Adam received an MFA in Media Arts Production from the University at Buffalo Department of Media Study. He has previously completed a Graduate Certificate in Screen Music from Australian Film, Television and Radio School (AFTRS) and a Bachelor of Information Technology (Data Communications) from Queensland University of Technology.
