LOOPBACK (2014)

Loopback is a suite of works revolving around exposing the packet structures of data communications protocols. Packetization and encapsulation processes are expressed as temporally expanded rhythmic elements. Works in the Loopback suite utilize controlled feedback, video and extreme vocal performance.
